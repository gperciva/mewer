MEWER 0.6: Rhythm Game

Flash Version
(includes microphone support)		 Requires Flash 9 or higher, and Javascript enabled. Microphone required for clapping, but you can still tap the SPACE bar instead.
Javascript Version
(no microphone)		 Works in Firefox and Safari; might not work in Internet Explorer.
Microphone:
If you don't have one, or don't want to enable microphone permission, you can hit SPACE instead of clapping or tapping. It's more fun when you make noise, though.
Gathering silence:
Don't try to be quiet here -- make whatever noises you make normally. Make your chair squeak, talk to your family, leave the dishwasher turned on, etc. Claps are generally much louder than normal household noise.
